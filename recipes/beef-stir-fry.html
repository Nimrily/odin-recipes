Beef Stir Fry

Home

Description

A stir fry using thin strips of beef sirloin, broccoli, bell pepper, and carrots. Topped with a garlic sesame soy sauce. Over steamed white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin
  • 1 1/2 cups broccoli
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon sesame seed

Steps

  1. cut beef into 2 inch strips and set a side
  2. cut carrot into match sticks
  3. cut bell pepper into strips
  4. use oil or butter in pan on medium high
  5. cook garlic for 30 sec or until fragrant
  6. toss beef wile stiring constantly
  7. then toss in corrot and bell pepper
  8. when beef is brown and corrots are tender
  9. toss in the soy sauce and seasme seeds
  10. sirfry for another minute or two
  11. serve over steamed white rice
