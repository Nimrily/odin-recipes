Red Beans and Rice

Description

A simple red bean and rice dish using canned beans and packaged sasuage. This dish cuts down on time and complexity while persivering flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup rice
  • 16 oz of sausage links
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 cans kindey beans
  • 1 large can of whole pealled tomatoes
  1. add washed rice and water to sauce pan
  2. bring pan to a biol
  3. cover and reduce heat
  4. while rice is cooking cut garlic,onion, and pepper
  5. suate onion and garlic in pan until franget
  6. add sausge and peppers in and cook till browned and tender then set aside
  7. in the same pan put the two drained cans of beans in on medium high
  8. add in the can of tomatoes and let it come to a boil
  9. add back in the peppers and sausage
  10. let it reduce untill the rice is done
  11. plate and enjoy
